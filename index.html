Common Questions

I've never trekked before. Can I come?

Absolutely. That's why I'm here. We will pick a route that matches your current fitness level, and I'll help you prepare with a workout plan before we leave.

What gear do I need to buy?

Less than you think! I will provide a checklist. You mostly need good shoes and warm layers. We can rent sleeping bags and tents if needed.

How is this different from a tour agency?

Agencies have fixed dates and rigid schedules. We are friends on a trip. If we like a village, we stay an extra day. If you want to stop for photos, we stop. It's your adventure.

Is it safe for solo female travelers?

Safety is the priority. We use only trusted homestays and verified routes. Since I customize every trip, we can discuss specific safety concerns you might have during planning.
