Moderate   |   5 Days

The Journey

This isn't a race. It's a week-long immersion into the quietest parts of Sikkim and Darjeeling, staying in homestays and walking through ancient rhododendron forests.

Highlights

  • Sunrise over Kanchenjunga from Tumling and Dhotrey.
  • Staying with a local families.
  • The rhododendron forests in full bloom if its the season.
