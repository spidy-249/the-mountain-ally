Why We Climb

By The Mountain Ally

People often ask me why I leave the comfort of the city for the harsh winds of the Himalayas. The answer isn't simple.

The Struggle is the Point

When you are at 14,000 feet, and your lungs are burning, you can't fake who you are. The pretenses of city life fall away. You become raw. You become real.

In my business, I don't guide you to the top so you can take a selfie. I guide you there so you can meet yourself.
